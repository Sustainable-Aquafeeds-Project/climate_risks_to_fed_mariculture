1. Select key fed mariculture species/groups based on ranked global production and existing maps of mariculture locations [1,2]. 
	- Allocate farms to species of interest using SST suitability (rough estimate per species, list of potential species)

2. Create standardised feeds for each species/group and determine major crops/fish for raw material demand. The range of raw material proportions will further provide a scope of possible feed compositions for the later risk assessment.

3. Select socio-economic pathways (SSPs) of interest, and standardise data for:
	3a. Crop yields and fished biomass through time for raw material demand
	3b. Sea surface temperature for production
	3c. Habitat suitability for mariculture adaptability
